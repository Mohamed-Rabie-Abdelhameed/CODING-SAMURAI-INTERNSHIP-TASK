How Does it Work?

TickIt is designed with simplicity and functionality in mind, making it effortless to manage your tasks and stay organized. Here's how it works:

1. Add Your Tasks

Add tasks to your list by clicking the "Add Task" button. Enter a title, description, and priority level for each task.

2. Organize with Priority

Sort your tasks by priority level to stay on top of what matters most. TickIt lets you sort your tasks by priority (high, medium, low) to stay on top of what matters most.

3. Edit and Manage

Edit your tasks by clicking the "Edit" button. You can also delete tasks by clicking the "Delete" button. TickIt makes it easy to manage your tasks.

4. Sort and Filter Tasks

Filter tasks by their status (completed or pending) to focus on what's currently relevant. TickIt puts you in the driver's seat.
